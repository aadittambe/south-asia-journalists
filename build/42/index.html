G. Raja Sekhar

Year: 1997

Nationality: India

Killed by: Political Group

Location: Hyderabad, India

Press organization: Eenadu Television (E-TV)

What's G. Raja Sekhar's story?

Raja Sekhar, an assistant for E-TV; Jagadish Babu, a producer for private channel E-TV; Gangadhara Raju, an E-TVcameraman; and Srinivas Rao and S. Krishna, assistant cameramen for E-TV, were killed in a car bomb explosionwhile covering the making of a film.
