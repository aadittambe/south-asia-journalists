What's Tahir Awan's story?

A suicide bomber killed Imran, a cameraman trainee forExpress TV, and Tahir Awan, a freelance reporter for the local Eitedal and ApnaAkhbar newspapers, in North West Frontier Province, now known as Khyber Pakhtunkhwa,according to the Pakistan Federal Union of Journalists and local news reports.The fatal explosion occurred in the wake of a smaller blastand apparently was intended to target the early responders to the scene, thereports said. At least five other people were killed and several more injured,including police and civilians, in the early evening attack, according to thenews reports.
