What's Razzaq Gul's story?

Gul, 35, a senior reporter with Express News TV in Turbat, acity in the Kech district of Baluchistan, was abducted near his home on theevening of May 18, according to news reports. His body was found the next daywith several bullet wounds and marks that indicated he had been tortured, hisfamily told local journalists. Gul was a member of the Baluch National Movement, anationalist political organization, and was the secretary of the Press Club ofTurbat, according to news reports.
