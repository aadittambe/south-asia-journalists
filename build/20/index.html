What's Aslam Durrani's story?

Durrani, 55, news editor of the Urdu-language DailyPakistan, was killed in a suicide bomb attack during a political rally forthe Awami National Party in the northwestern provincial capital of Peshawar,according to news reports. His death was confirmed to CPJ by a staff member atthe Lahore offices of the paper by phone. News reports also identified him as Tariq Aslam Durrani.News accounts reportedthat more than 20 others were killed in the attack, which occurred in the denselypopulated neighborhood of Yakatoot. The explosion occurred just after thearrival of Awami National Party leaders, The Associated Press reported. Seniorpolice official Shafqat Malik told AFPthat the attack was a suicide bombing.Two other journalists were injured: Ehtesham Khan, acorrespondent for Express TV, and Azhar Ali Shah, a reporter for DailyPakistan, both of whom were taken to a local hospital for treatment. Haroon Bilour survived the attack.Durrani was survived by his wife and four sons.
