Veeraboina Yadagiri

Year: 2004

Nationality: India

Killed by: Criminal Group

Location: Medak, India

Press organization: Andhra Prabha

What's Veeraboina Yadagiri's story?

Yadagiri, a veteran journalist and staff correspondent for the local,Telugu-language daily Andhra Prabha, was stabbed to death nearhis home in the town of Medak, in India’s southern Andhra PradeshState. Local journalists told CPJ that Yadagiri, 35, was murderedin reprisal for his articles investigating the illegal sale of home-brewedliquor, known locally as toddy.
