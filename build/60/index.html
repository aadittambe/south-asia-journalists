Jamal Uddin

Year: 2012

Nationality: Bangladesh

Killed by: Criminal Group

Location: Kashipur, Bangladesh

Press organization: Gramer Kagoj

What's Jamal Uddin's story?

An unidentified group of men armed with sharp weapons,including machetes, attacked Uddin at a tea stall in Kashipur village in thedistrict of Jessore on the border with India, according to news reports. Theassailants slashed his arms, legs, and eyes, leaving him with multiple severeinjuries, news reports said. Uddin died at a local hospital later that night,the reports said.
