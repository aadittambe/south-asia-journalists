What's Abdul Wahab's story?

Abdul Wahab, a reporter for Express News, and Pervez Khan, aWaqt TV journalist, were among 50 people killed in a double-suicide bomb attackin the Mohmand tribal district, according to international news reports. The journalists were covering a meeting of tribal leadersand government officials in Ghalanai, the administrative center of the region,when two suicide bombers wearing police uniforms detonated explosives. Newsreports said the meeting was called to discuss the formation of an anti-Talibanmilitia.
