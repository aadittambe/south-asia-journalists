Siraj Uddin

Year: 2008

Nationality: Pakistan

Killed by:

Location: Mingora, Pakistan

Press organization: The Nation

What's Siraj Uddin's story?

Uddin died in a suicide bombing that took the lives of morethan 40 people, according to Pakistani news reports. No organization claimedresponsibility for the attack, which occurred at the funeral of a slain policeofficer and wounded about 80 people, including two other journalists.
