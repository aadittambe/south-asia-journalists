What's Siraj Uddin's story?

Uddin died in a suicide bombing that took the lives of morethan 40 people, according to Pakistani news reports. No organization claimedresponsibility for the attack, which occurred at the funeral of a slain policeofficer and wounded about 80 people, including two other journalists.The Swat Valley was a focal point of conflict at the time.Militants had taken over much of the area in 2007, and government forces werereasserting some control by early 2008.
