Faisal Qureshi

Year: 2011

Nationality: Pakistan

Killed by: Political Group

Location: Lahore, Pakistan

Press organization: The London Post

What's Faisal Qureshi's story?

The body of Qureshi, 31, an editor for the political newswebsite The London Post, was discovered about 2 a.m. by his brother Zahid afterfamily members found blood stains outside the journalist’s house, the Pakistanidaily The Express Tribune reported. Police reports said Qureshi’s throat hadbeen cut and described his body as showing signs of torture, The ExpressTribune said.
